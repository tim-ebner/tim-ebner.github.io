# Privacy Policy for Quiz Gallery

**Last updated:** January 22, 2026

Tim Ebner ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Android application **Quiz Gallery** (the "App").

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting it.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**Quiz Gallery does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data.**

The App only accesses **locally stored media files** (photos) on your device to read **metadata information** (such as image-related details) for functionality purposes.  
All processing happens **entirely on your device**.

- No data is sent to a server
- No data is shared with third parties
- No data is stored externally

---

## 2. Permissions Used

The App requests the following Android permission:

- **Storage / Media Access**  
  This permission is required to access images stored on your device so the App can function correctly.

The App does **not** access, collect, or upload your files beyond local processing.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

Quiz Gallery does **not** use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising services, or crash reporting services.

The App may provide **external links**, such as a link to Google Maps, but no data is shared automatically when using these links. Any interaction with external services is subject to their own privacy policies.

---

## 4. Advertising & Monetization

- The App contains **no advertisements**
- The App has **no in-app purchases**
- The App has **no subscriptions**
- The App is **completely free to use**

---

## 5. User Accounts

- No account registration is required
- No login or authentication data is collected

---

## 6. Data Storage & Retention

- No personal data is stored
- No user data is retained
- No backend or cloud storage is used

All information accessed by the App remains on your device.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Quiz Gallery is intended for a **general audience** and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children, including children under the age of 13.

Because no data is collected, the App complies with applicable children’s privacy regulations such as **COPPA**.

---

## 8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.  
Since the App does not transmit or store user data, the risk of data exposure is minimized.

---

Because Quiz Gallery does not collect or process personal data, users do not need to exercise data access, correction, or deletion rights.
